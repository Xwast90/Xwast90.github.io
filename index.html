Flexbox

Overall Flexbox feels, both easier and harder to work with then Skeleton. Easier in that it has simple classes as opposed to multiple, but at the same time, I had several issues with the scaling and rearranging of objects, though I can certainly say the ability to change the order of objects depending on the size is quite interesting. I can say I love the way that it scales overal,however I don't understand the fact of how when I viewed the site on my phone, it failed to show the correct version.
